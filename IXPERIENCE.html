PROTOTYPE

(OVERVIEW)

iXperience is a game app that invites users to actively engage with their favorite brands to earn real-world rewards. They explore the physical world through billions of location-anchored, interaction points, activating branded AR experiences, global competition and play-to-earn mini-games.

VISION

(THE GOAL)

The company needed a redesign of their existing product “Seek Rewards” to iXperience.


(THE CHALLENGE)

“We need users to navigate the app easily”


The visuals portrayed from their existing design failed in communicating the experience users are required to interact with in a modern model of a game app.

LOW-FIDELITY DESIGN

(WIREFRAMING)

Redesigning the app required looking at loopholes from the previous version and understanding how better we could create a seamless experience for users.


(KEY FACTOR)

Modernizing was a key factor in making decisions as we needed to give the existing design a facelift that attributes to what functionalities the new version is to carry out.
